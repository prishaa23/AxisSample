Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Mayonnaise and Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • Mayonnaise
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Carrors,Tomatoes,Avacadoes and Olivies
  • 4 slices of bread
  • Butter, for spreading

Instructions:

  1. Place the cheese slices on two slices of bread. Top with Mayonnaise and vegetables. Cover with the remaining bread slices.
  2. Spread butter on both sides of the sandwiches.
  3. Heat a frying pan over medium-high heat. Place the sandwiches in the pan and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  4. Serve hot.
